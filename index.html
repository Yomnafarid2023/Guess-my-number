Rules of the Game

  • Under the question mark (?) there's a hidden number. Starting with score of (20), you will try to guess that hidden number by entering a number between 1 & 20 . After each Check you will get a clue if your number is lower or higher than the hidden number.
  • After each wrong guess the score decreases by (1) until you guess the right number.If you reach (0) in the score, you lose.
  • The highscore will reflect the last score you get after all the wrong guesses.
  • If you wish to play again just click the (Again) button and it will reset everything but your highscore.
